The Best Animes According to KLOP

My Top 3 Animes

Jujutsu Kaisen

This is my favourite anime.
It is a mystery anime that has a plot about a high school boy who got possessed by a "demon"(thereby receiving some supernatural powers) because he swallowed a sealed curse.

That time I got Reincarnated as a slime

This is my second best anime.
This is also a mystery anime that has a plot about a guy that was killed while trying to save his mentee.
He got reincarnated into another world as a slime who earned a lot of abilities through his adventures.

My Hero Academia

This is an action anime about a world of people with different abilities divided into classes of heroes and villians.
